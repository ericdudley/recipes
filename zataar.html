Za’atar

  • 1 loaf
  • 25 minutes
  • Sweet

Za’atar spread is a flavorful Middle Eastern mixture of herbs and oil, perfect for slathering on dough to make a fragrant and savory bread.

  • 1/2 cup za’atar (Middle Eastern spice mixture)
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 1 ball dough
  • baking sheet

Ingredients

Preheat oven to 425 °F.

  • za’atar
  • oil

Mix za’atar and oil together in a small bowl.

  • za’atar-oil mixture
  • dough

Spread the za’atar-oil mixture onto dough.

Bake at 425 °F for 20 minutes.

Enjoy!
